Windsor Fitness project

Windsor Sports Group, Fitness, cycling and fitness business.
Company that only had a B2B, and needed a B2C store that covers all its brands in one place.
The type of users who would buy online, all the variants of products that could exist in inventory, the limitations of the SAP platform vs. the needs of the client, business benchmarks, and other variables were investigated.
Made to be easy to read, communicate through images and help text. Clean design making the images the protagonists.

Guinot Colombia

Guinot Colombia. Beauty and skincare.
Design based on beauty skin care, using the institutional color red and gold for the important or action elements for the user.
I tried to highlight the beauty and natural glow of the skin using models of different age ranges, using light-colored backgrounds and clothes so as not to distract from what is important.
The structure has a lot of margin, the information is divided by headlines and light backgrounds are used to emphasize the images and give prominence to the most important elements.
